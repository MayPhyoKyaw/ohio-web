Company Profile

OHIO Myanmar International Co., Ltd. was established in 2014. It is developed to strengthen education, economy and national development with six departments, such as the Department of International Business, the Department of Services (overseas business and employment consultant), the Department of Import and Export, the Department of Commerce Wholesaler, the CSR Department, the Education Department.
